About Author

Aadesh Verma is the author of Agony and Ecstasy. His work has been highly appreciated. He has spent his childhood in Raipur-C.G. After completing his MBA in marketing form Pune he worked in media houses and telecom major in Pune. He is a motivational speaker and a certified master trainer by N.B.I. on whole brain thinking and by TSL on O.B.T. . He is the founder director of Confluence College and Confluence Military School. He is inclined towards instrumental music and he loves teaching and travelling. The best way to connect with him is through his official page or you can drop an e-mail on author@aadeshverma.com.
